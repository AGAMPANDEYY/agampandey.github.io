Agam Pandey

AI Researcher • Applied AI Engineer

|

Hi! I’m Agam Pandey, a senior undergrad at IIT Roorkee. I do applied AI research on production AI systems: information retrieval, Evals and benchmarking, and multimodal safety/robustness (VLM reasoning, adversarial ML, agents, and vision). I’m currently an Applied AI Research Intern at Mem0 and previously built data/AI systems at Swiggy, Akki AI, EzAIx, Mindshift Analytics, and Strabag Efkon India, while co-authoring work at NeurIPS-RML/MLRC/TMLR/AAAI-SAPP. I’m also a Research Intern at Transportation Lab, IIT Jodhpur with Prof. Akshay Gupta on multimodal sensor fusion and collision-warning systems. Outside work, I run middle/long distance for Athletics Team IIT Roorkee and contribute to Data Science Group.
