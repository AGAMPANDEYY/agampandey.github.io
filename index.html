Hi! I’m Agam Pandey, a final-year undergrad at IIT Roorkee and i work in turning research into reliable and practical AI systems. I work in AI Safety, Robustness and Interpretability in Multimodal models, Causal Reasoning in VLMs, Adversarial ML, Graph Embeddings for retrievals, AI Agents and Computer Vision for Autonomous Perception. In the past i have worked on both Data Science algorithms, desiging and building complex AI Systems during internships at Swiggy, Akki AI, EzAIx, Mindshift Analytics and Strabag Efkon India , while co-authoring work at NeurIPS-RML/MLRC/TMLR/AAAI-SAPP. I am also a Research Intern at Transportation Lab, IIT Jodhpur supervised by Prof. Akshay Gupta working on Multimodal Sensor Fusion and Collsion Warning systems. When I’m not coding or researching, you’ll find me running 1500m, 5000m and marathons for Athletics Team IIT Roorkee or contributing to student tech group, Data Science Group.
