Agam Pandey

AI Researcher • AI Engineer

Hi! I’m Agam Pandey, a final-year undergrad at IIT Roorkee and i work in turning research into reliable and practical AI systems. My interests include AI Safety Robustness and Interpretability in Multimodal models, Causal Reasoning in VLMs, Adversarial ML, Graph Embeddings for retrievals, AI Agents, hybrid-RAG, and Computer Vision for Autonomous Perception. I’ve built multi-agent assistants, Hybrid-RAG pipelines, and production automations during internships at Swiggy, Akki AI, EzAIx and Mindshift Analytics, while co-authoring work at NeurIPS-RML/MLRC/AAAI SAPP. I am also a Research Intern at Transportation Lab, IIT Jodhpur supervised by Prof. Akshay Gupta. Off the laptop, I’m a distance athlete for IIT Roorkee (1500m/5000m) and an active contributor to student tech communities.
